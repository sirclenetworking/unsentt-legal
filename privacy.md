Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

## What We Collect
- A random anonymous ID stored on your device and our servers
- Posts you create
- How long you view posts (used to rank content)
- Reports you submit

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifying information.

## How We Use It
- To show your posts in the Mine section
- To rank content in the feed based on engagement
- To enforce our content policy (reports and flags)

## Data Storage
Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers. You can read Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Deletion
To request deletion of your data, email support@cocial.in
